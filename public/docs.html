Citizen Guidelines

Truthful Reporting

Report only factual details. False reporting may lead to legal action.

Emergency Priority

Call 100/112 first for immediate danger. Use online FIR for non-emergency cases.

Evidence Quality

Upload clear photos or scans for quicker verification.

Security & Privacy

Data Minimization

Only essential data is collected for FIR processing.

Restricted Access

Only authorized officers can access case information.

Account Safety

Never share OTPs. Always verify the official website domain.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take?

Typical verification takes 24–48 hours depending on case volume.

Can I update my FIR?

Changes can be requested via the contact helpdesk with your FIR ID.

Is digital signature valid?

Yes, it is used to confirm identity and submission intent.
